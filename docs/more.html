Meet the Inventors

Vint Cerf "The Father of Internet"

Vinton Gray Cerf is an American Internet Poineer sharing his title with TCP/IP co-developer Bob Kahn

Bob Kahn

Robert Elliot kahn is an American electrical engineer, who along with Vint Cerf, first proposed the transmission Control Protocol and the Internet Protocol, the fundamental communication protocols at the heart of the Internet.

Tim Berners-lee

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He is a Professorial Fellow of Computer Science at the University of Oxford and a professor at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology
